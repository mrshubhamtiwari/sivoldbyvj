Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

At SIVOLD by Verma Jewellers, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The info includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, phone number (used for OTP authentication), and email address (optional).
  • Transactional Data: Details about payments (processed securely via gateways), billing addresses, and order history.
  • Device Information: Device ID, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure app security and compatibility.
  • Customer Support Data: Information you provide when contacting us for help, including chat messages and images of products or issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following specific purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process your orders, manage your account, and deliver jewellery to your doorstep.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity via SMS OTP to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Communication: To send you order updates, invoices, and promotional offers (which you can opt-out of).
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage trends and crash logs to improve app performance and user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We strictly share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Targeted third-party services like Google Firebase (for secure hosting and database) and payment gateways (for processing transactions).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. All data transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

To Delete Your Account: You can delete your account directly within the app by navigating to Profile > Delete Account. This action permanently removes your personal data from our active databases.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
