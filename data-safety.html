Data Safety & Privacy

Transparent reporting on how we collect, share, and manage your data.

1. Data Collection

The SIVOLD app collects the following user data to provide our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, Phone Number (for OTP authentication), and Email Address (optional, for profile).
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and diagnostics to improve app stability.
  • Device or other IDs: Device identifiers for account security and fraud prevention.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following third-party services required for app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication, database hosting, and analytics.
  • Expo: For delivering over-the-air updates and push notifications.

3. Security Practices

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is managed via secure OTP (One-Time Password) protocols.

4. Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to be forgotten. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the application.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the SIVOLD mobile application.
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right icon).
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the menu.
  4. Tap on Delete Account (highlighted in red).
  5. Confirm your choice in the popup dialog.

Note: This action is irreversible. It will permanently remove your profile, order history, and saved addresses from our servers.

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by emailing us at sivold.vj@gmail.com with your registered phone number.
